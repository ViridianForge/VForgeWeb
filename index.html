Wayne "ViridianForge" Manselle

Programmer, Writer, Chiptune Enthusiast

Having received my M.S. in Computer and Information Science from the University of Oregon, I began my career developing software to collect and analyse scientific data in an academic research environment in 2007 at Dr. Marjorie Woollacott's Motor Control and Cognition Lab. My work there has has been a wonderful challenge, exposing me to a wide variety of experimental paradigms in the field of Human Physiology.

My spare time is split between volunteering with the amazing folks at Chiptunes=WIN as a writer, editor and developer of cool tools. As a team, we raise awareness of awesome music and events in the Chiptune community.
